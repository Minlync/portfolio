User Research for Craigslist App and Redesign Craigslist Housing App.

Utilitarian VS Aesthetics

Craigslist is an American classified advertisements website and established in 1995 by founder Craig Newmark. The original intention of Newmark that he want to closing the distance of people, and create a nice interpersonal relationship through from internet. Over by the time, the number of users is growing. Likewise, the function of Craigslist also expending in order to fit users’ demands. In the new version of Craigslist, I separate the housing function from the main App. Then, it is becoming an individual App.

People might think question in distinct perspective. For example, businessmen might focus on the question “how to increasing the profit or how to expanding user group”. However, a designer may more care about the aesthetics and exterior. In the aspect of UI designer, craigslist is old. However, UX designer may want to improve it more from utilitarian perspective. How to ensure a good user experience while still being beautiful has become a challenge.
